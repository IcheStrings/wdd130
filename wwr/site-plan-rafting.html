Overview

purpose

To attract new customers, provide information about offerings, build brand awareness, enable bookings, and expand the company's reach beyond its local area.

Audience

Who are the target customers? The target customers are adventure-seeking individuals and families, typically between the ages of 25 and 55, with a moderate to high disposable income and an interest in outdoor recreational activities.
What do they want? They want information on the different rafting trip options, including difficulty levels, duration, special features, and pricing. They also want to see visuals like photos and videos to get a sense of the experience.
How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? The audience will likely access the website using a variety of devices, including desktop computers, laptops, and mobile devices, so the website should be designed to be responsive and user-friendly across different platforms.
